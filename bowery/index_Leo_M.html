A friendly reminder to volunteer.

The Bowery Mission knows its supporters are commited to bringing hope and opportunity to the men, women and children of New York City who are temporarily down on their luck. And it is largely through your financial contributions that make temporarily the operative word.


The Bowery Mission hopes to call on its wonderful supporters once again. This time, we are asking for a contribution not of your money, but of your time. Please read on to learn of the myriad ways you can, once again, change someone's life.

The Medical Clinic is always seeking doctors, nurse practitioners, registered nurses, optometrists and social workers to serve the homeless and uninsured patients on Wednesday evenings at the Bowery Mission.

There are a number of opportunities to mentor 13-16 year olds who are a part of the Leadership Academy. A caring adult who is willing to positively share faith and values can make all the difference.

Many of our volunteers have special skills and gifts. If you have a professional skill, area of special knowledge or even a hobby you would like to share with our management or those we serve, please let us know.

“Whatever you did for the least of these brothers of mine, you did for me.”
